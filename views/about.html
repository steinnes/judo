About

JudoCal.com is a site intended for Judo trainers across Europe to find tournaments, training camps and other Judo-related events in their neighbouring countries and sourroundings. Every good athlete knows that a key ingredient to success is to compete, often and against new and stronger opponents. JudoCal.com aims to provide the platform where those seeking that experience can be brought together.
